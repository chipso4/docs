## What this hub does

The Warranty Claims Hub is the manufacturer-facing side of the warranty process. Unlike the Returns and Warranty hubs (which talk to a distributor's ERP), this hub connects directly to the **OEM or manufacturer's warranty system** (e.g., Rheem/Ruud, Daikin).

It participates in two phases of the broader [warranty event](/warranty-hub/warranty-flow): Phase 1 (identify the defect) and Phase 3 (file the claim). It can also operate standalone for manufacturers who only need claim processing.

## Integration direction

This is a **reverse integration** — the manufacturer builds the middleware API, and Continuum calls it.

```
Continuum → Manufacturer middleware → ERP / Warranty DB / PIM
```

## Endpoints (flow order)

**Phase 1 — Identify the defect** *(before replacement order)*

| | Method | Endpoint | Purpose | Notes |
|-|--------|----------|---------|-------|
| 1 | GET | [`/validate-unit`](/api-reference/unit-data/validate-unit) | Validate serial, get model | *Only if brand supports serial lookup* |
| 2 | GET | [`/bill-of-materials`](/api-reference/parts/bill-of-materials) | Parts list for the unit | Customer selects failed part |
| 3 | GET | [`/claim-types`](/warranty-claims-hub/implementation-checklist) | Available claim types | Per serial/model |
| 4 | GET | [`/products/replacements`](/api-reference/parts/replacements) | Replacement part options | Handles superseded parts |
| 5 | GET | [`/products/reason-codes`](/api-reference/parts/warranty-codes) | Failure reason codes | Part-specific |

*→ Flow moves to [Phase 2 (Warranty Hub)](/warranty-hub/warranty-flow#phase-2-replace-the-part) for the replacement order.*

**Phase 3 — File the warranty claim** *(after replacement order invoiced)*

| | Method | Endpoint | Purpose |
|-|--------|----------|---------|
| 6 | POST | `/claim/validate` | Pre-validate claim (dry run) |
| 7 | POST | `/claim` | Submit the warranty claim |
| 8 | POST | `/claim/upload-attachment` | Upload photos / documents |
| 9 | GET | `/claim/status` | Poll for manufacturer decision |
| 10 | PATCH | `/claim` | Update claim (if corrections needed) |
| 11 | DELETE | `/claim` | Cancel a claim |

*→ Flow moves to [Phase 4 (Warranty Hub)](/warranty-hub/warranty-flow#phase-4-handle-the-defective-part) for vendor return, then [Phase 5 (Returns Hub)](/warranty-hub/warranty-flow#phase-5-credit-the-customer) for credit.*

**Reference data**

| | Method | Endpoint | Purpose |
|-|--------|----------|---------|
| 12 | GET | [`/locations`](/api-reference/reference-data/locations) | Warranty service locations |
| 13 | GET | [`/corporations`](/api-reference/reference-data/corporations) | Authorized distributors |
| 14 | GET | [`/corporations/details`](/api-reference/reference-data/corporation-details) | Distributor details |
| 15 | GET | [`/corporations/customers`](/api-reference/reference-data/corporation-customers) | Customers under a distributor |
| 16 | GET | [`/contractors`](/api-reference/reference-data/contractors) | Contractor directory |
| 17 | GET | [`/labor-rates`](/api-reference/reference-data/labor-rates) | Labor/travel rate schedule |

**System**

| | Method | Endpoint | Purpose |
|-|--------|----------|---------|
| 18 | POST | [`/oauth2/token`](/getting-started/authentication) | Get access token |
| 19 | GET | `/health` | Health check |

<Info>
All endpoints use the V2 base: `/api/btu/v2/`. Claim lifecycle endpoints (6–11) are documented in the [Claim flow](/warranty-claims-hub/claim-flow) and [Status lifecycle](/warranty-claims-hub/status-lifecycle) pages.
</Info>
